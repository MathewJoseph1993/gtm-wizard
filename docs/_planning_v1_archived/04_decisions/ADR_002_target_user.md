# ADR-002: Target User Decision

> **Status:** Pending Research
> **Decision Date:** TBD
> **Last Updated:** December 25, 2024

## Context

GTM Wizard needs to define its primary target user to focus development and positioning.

## Options Considered

### Option A: Solo GTM Engineers/Founders
- Individual practitioners
- Technical, comfortable with CLI/MCP
- Price sensitive, self-serve

### Option B: SMB GTM Teams (2-10 people)
- Small teams coordinating outbound
- Mix of technical and non-technical
- Moderate budget

### Option C: Enterprise RevOps Teams
- Large organizations
- Complex tech stacks
- Enterprise pricing possible

### Option D: AI Agent Developers
- Developers building GTM automation
- Highly technical
- Need expertise layer for agents

## Decision Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Pain severity | 25% | How much do they suffer? |
| Willingness to pay | 25% | Budget, history of buying |
| Accessibility | 20% | Can we reach them? |
| Market size | 15% | How many exist? |
| Our expertise fit | 15% | Do we understand them? |
